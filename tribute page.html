Arnold Schwarzenegger

An Austrian-American actor, former bodybuilder, producer, businessman, and former politician who served as the 38th governor of California from 2003 to 2011.

Here's a time line of Arnold life:

  • Born July 30, 1947
  • 1962 - started lifting weights
  • 1968 - emigrated to USA
  • 1969 - won Mr. Universe title
  • 1984 - played title character in "Terminator"
  • 2003 - became the 38th Governor of the State of California
  • 2011 - co-founded the R20 Regions of Climate Action, a global non-profit dedicated to helping subnational governments develop, implement, and communicate the importance of low-carbon and climate resilient projects as well as their economic benefits
  • 2012 - recognized as a 2012 Global Advocate by the United Nations Correspondents Association for his work with the organization
  • 2021 - and still living
